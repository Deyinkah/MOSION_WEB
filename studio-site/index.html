The Vision
01

Cinema is moving
to the palm.

African cinema is among the most vital storytelling traditions on earth - yet it has been constrained by theater infrastructure that was never built for its scale. MOSION Studio is the infrastructure that was missing.

We believe the next generation of Nollywood and Ghallywood premieres won't open in Lagos theaters or Accra multiplexes - they'll premiere on the device in your pocket, reaching millions on day one.

Reach alone is not enough. MOSION is not an open upload site - it is a curated mobile-first cinema, and every partner and every title must meet the standard before release.

Partner Standards

A cinema standard,
not an open upload lane.

MOSION Studio is for approved producers, distributors, and rights holders. Studio access does not guarantee listing; every title is reviewed against MOSION's technical, creative, rights, and release standards.

Selection Signals

What makes an
application credible.

The strongest applications show clear rights control, a serious release plan, and a package that already looks ready for a premium audience-facing premiere.

Unclear rights, unfinished delivery materials, placeholder packaging, or mass-upload intent usually do not move forward.

01

Rights Package

Clear ownership or distribution authority, with a credible basis to release the title on MOSION.

02

Release Intent

A defined audience, territory, and release objective instead of a generic upload request.

03

Delivery Readiness

Picture, sound, metadata, artwork, trailer, and supporting assets should already be moving toward release quality.

04

Catalog Fit

MOSION is built for cinema-standard features, premium documentaries, and event-ready titles, not bulk catalog dumps.
