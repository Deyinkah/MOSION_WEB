The Vision
01

Cinema is moving
to the palm.

African cinema is among the most vital storytelling traditions on earth - yet it has been constrained by theater infrastructure that was never built for its scale. MOSION Studio is the infrastructure that was missing.

We believe the next generation of Nollywood and Ghallywood premieres won't open in Lagos theaters or Accra multiplexes - they'll premiere on the device in your pocket, reaching millions on day one.

And for the first time, filmmakers will know exactly what they earned - the moment they earn it.

The Platform

Built for filmmakers
& distributors.

Professional-grade tools and technology designed specifically for cinema distribution on mobile - not adapted from something else.

Secure DRM Streaming
Industry-standard digital rights management prevents piracy and keeps your content protected from day one.
Mobile-First Cinema
Optimized for smartphone viewing - your film delivered in cinema quality to the device your audience already carries.
Cinema-Split Revenue Model
Mosion operates on the same 60/40 and 70/30 revenue splits used by traditional cinemas - but with one major difference: you see every naira in real time. No delayed statements. No unexplained deductions. Full transparency from first play to final withdrawal.
Flexible Release Windows
Choose your premiere timing and distribution schedule. Run pre-orders before launch to build audience momentum.
Real-Time Earnings Dashboard
Your dashboard shows live data on purchases, views, and revenue - broken down by title. Withdraw your earnings directly from the platform whenever you're ready. No third party between you and your money.
Submission Support
Guided review and approval process so your film is set up for success from upload through premiere.
Free to Distribute
There are no upfront fees to list your film on MOSION. Submit, get reviewed, and premiere at zero cost. Revenue sharing only begins when your audience starts watching.
