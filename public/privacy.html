01 Scope

What this page covers

This policy covers personal information processed through the public MOSION marketing site, the public waitlist, the MOSION Studio landing page, the Studio application form, and the Studio web app when it is accessed through the Studio host.

It does not replace separate commercial, partner, onboarding, or platform terms that may apply once a filmmaker, distributor, rights holder, or partner is approved for additional Studio access.

02 Information We Collect

Information we collect

The information collected depends on how you interact with MOSION. Some information is submitted directly by you, some is created through account use, and some technical information may be processed automatically as part of operating, securing, and improving the service.

Public waitlist and contact information

On the public MOSION site, you may submit your email address to join the waitlist or request updates about launch access, beta access, or product announcements tied to the MOSION service.

Studio application information

When you apply for MOSION Studio access, you may provide your first name, last name, email address, website or portfolio URL, company, slate, or lead-title information, applicant type, rights status, release stage, territory focus, trailer or screener URL, and any submission summary or supporting context you choose to include.

Account, authentication, and profile information

When you sign in to the Studio web app, request a password reset, restore a session, or manage your profile, we may process your email address, password or password-reset inputs, authentication status, provider information, session-related account details, and profile data such as first name, last name, producer or studio name, title, phone number, website, and studio biography.

Organisation and partner relationship information

We may also process information connected to your organisation, studio, distribution relationship, or rights-holder relationship where that is needed to review access, administer partner accounts, or operate Studio workflows.

Technical and usage information

Hosting, infrastructure, and security systems may process technical information such as browser or device details, IP-related request data, timestamps, page requests, error events, and session-validation or abuse-prevention data associated with use of the site.

05 Cookies And Browser Storage

Cookies and browser storage currently in use

MOSION uses limited first-party cookies and browser storage to support notice frequency control, returning-user routing, and Studio sign-in continuity on the same browser. The specific items currently in use include the following.

Cookie/local storage: mosion_prototype_notice_seen_v1

Used to remember that the public site preview notice has already been shown on that browser, so the same notice is not repeated unnecessarily. The public privacy page is intentionally excluded from that notice.

Type: first-party cookie plus local-storage fallback. Retention: up to 12 months. Purpose: notice frequency control.

Cookie: studio_known_user

Set after a Studio sign-in has been restored or completed successfully. It tells the Studio landing page that this browser has signed in before, so returning users can be sent straight to the Studio sign-in flow instead of always starting on the landing page.

Type: first-party cookie. Retention: up to 12 months. Purpose: returning-user routing only.

Cookie/local storage: mosion_studio_cookie_notice_seen_v1

Used only on the Studio landing page to remember that you dismissed the Studio cookie information banner, so the same message is not shown repeatedly after you have already seen it.

Type: first-party cookie plus local-storage fallback. Retention: up to 12 months. Purpose: banner dismissal state.

Local storage: mosion_auth_session_v1

Used by the Studio web app after sign-in to keep the active Studio session on the current browser. Clearing browser storage or signing out removes that remembered session.

Type: local storage. Retention: until sign-out or storage is cleared. Purpose: session continuity on this device.

12 Browser Settings

If cookies are blocked

The public site and the Studio landing page can still load if cookies are blocked, but some convenience behaviour may stop working. In particular, notice dismissal state may not be remembered, and returning Studio users may stay on the landing page instead of being sent straight to sign in.

In practical terms, disabling cookies does not stop the public pages from loading. It mainly removes the browser memory layer that helps MOSION avoid repeating notices and helps Studio recognise a returning browser for faster sign-in routing.
