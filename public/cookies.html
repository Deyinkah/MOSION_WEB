MOSION uses limited first-party browser storage on mosion.app to support notice frequency control and returning-user routing. No third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are used.

01 Storage In Use

Cookies and browser storage on mosion.app

The following first-party items are currently in use on mosion.app.

Cookie / local storage: mosion_prototype_notice_seen_v1

Used to remember that the public site preview notice has already been shown on that browser, so the same notice is not repeated unnecessarily. The public privacy page is intentionally excluded from that notice.

Type: first-party cookie plus local-storage fallback. Retention: up to 12 months. Purpose: notice frequency control.

02 If Cookies Are Blocked

If cookies are blocked

The public site can still load if cookies are blocked. In practical terms, disabling cookies mainly removes the browser memory layer that helps MOSION avoid repeating notices. It does not prevent the site from loading or functioning.
