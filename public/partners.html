How it works

From partner registration to launch and payout.

The supply-side journey is structured so studios, producers, and distributors understand exactly how films move from submission to audience access.

1

Register as a partner

Create your MOSION Studio partner account and define your organization profile.

2

Submit your movie

Upload title information, release details, and the material needed for listing review.

3

Approval process

The MOSION team reviews the submission, verifies readiness, and confirms launch eligibility.

4

Launch on MOSION Cinema

5

Track revenue

Monitor purchases, audience activity, and performance through the studio dashboard.

Withdraw earnings
